courier / monospaced

Cohesive and intentional design implementation is fundamental to effective design. Each element carries a rich history, from hierarchy and typography to color theory and beyond. I chose Courier as the primary font for Chrrybmb because of its nostalgic charm. It evokes memories of the Gameboy days when character scripts were rendered in monospaced typefaces, bringing a touch of retro appeal to modern design.

Color Palette

Courier is a classic monospaced font designed by Howard Kettler in 1955 for IBM typewriters. Known for its distinctive typewriter style, Courier ensures each character occupies the same width, making it perfect for coding, legal documents, and screenplays. Its clean, uniform appearance enhances readability and evokes a sense of timeless functionality. Whether for nostalgic appeal or practical use, Courier remains a go-to choice for many designers and professionals.
