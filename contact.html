Hi. My name is Denisa, and I am a self-taught digital designer and avid yogi. Over the past few years, I have completed various coding and design courses, but my passion for digital design began at the age of 12 when I started coding Xanga layouts using basic HTML to showcase my designs.

I officially began my journey in the creative industry over 12 years ago, gaining experience across diverse areas such as design, print, marketing, and ecommerce. However, eventually I knew I'd always find myself back in the world of coding, as it holds a special place in my heart for many reasons.

My expertise lies in digital design, encompassing front-end web design and development, graphic design, print, and more. I enjoy blending these diverse disciplines to create cohesive and engaging experiences.

When I'm not learning or creating, you'll find me in the studio practicing yoga.
